It's All In The Details

It’s All In The Details premier Finish and Trim Carpentry has been serving the New England area since 1993. We have an unmatched reputation for providing high quality craftsmanship at a reasonable cost. We are well known for our attention to detail and commitment to excellence.

Our staff has extensive knowledge in all aspects of carpentry and finish work, built‐in cabinetry, framing, siding, window/door installation, flooring installation, stairs, decks and roofs.

We work closely with owners, architects, designers, subcontractors, and structural engineers. We understand that making improvements to your home or business can easily become overwhelming and frustrating, but we are here to help. If you need a kitchen or bath remodeled or master bedroom suite designed and built, or just a few repairs done to your home, you can count on us to get the job done right, on time and on budget.
